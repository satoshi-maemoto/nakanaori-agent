# Teacher Brief Prompt

Generate a one-page brief for the homeroom teacher.

## Required sections

1. Timeline (brief events)
2. Child A: facts, feelings, unknowns
3. Child B: facts, feelings, unknowns
4. Agreements and disagreements (neutral language)
5. Unknowns requiring teacher confirmation
6. Suggested questions for the teacher (not decisions)

## Required disclaimer

Include: 「この整理はAIによるものです。最終的な判断は先生が行ってください。」

## Forbidden

Never recommend punishment, rewards, or who is at fault.
